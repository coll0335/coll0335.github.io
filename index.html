Layout Design

This project is a collaboration between myself and my brother, Jerick Collates, who took the photos. The objective of this project was to create the look and feel for a magazine that will showcase photography that pays homage/emulates a famous photographer. In this case, my brother picked Martin Munkácsi.

Ready Set Shop

A conceptual app that helps users get in and out of shopping centres as quickly as possible. The app parses information from the local WiFi connection of the centre and it only lists the shops that are currently having deals or sales. The user can also search by shops or products.

Steez TV

A motion graphics projects focused on creating a broadcast package for an existing channel/network or a fictional one. Steez specializes on showcasing TV shows that are about 'street' oriented activities.

Virtual Eyes

Virtualeyez enables artist to “mock up” their artwork on surrounding buildings, which are called canvases, to look as if they are painted on the canvas. By using your mobile device to act as a viewport, you can view each piece by hovering your device over the specified locations, revealing each artwork as if they are painted on the walls themselves.

Jerick's Website

Branding and creating a website for Jerick Collantes' photography website. The minimalistic approach does not distract the viewer from the actual work.

Project Nippon

Project Nippon’s aim is to take the Japanese Hannya Mask and overlay it famous statues/idols all over the world, by combing Japanese art with differing statues something new is born. The overarching goal of this project is to harmonize art styles, whether they are the similar or very different. Also, to inspire other artists and show my love for culture in general, not just a specific one. Because diversity makes the world an interesting place

Quote Book

Tao Te Ching is an accumulation of Chinese Proverbs. This booklet focuses on the the typographic aspect. The main goal was to harmonize hand done type and screen type. To complete the piece, I was inspired by traditional bamboo scrolls , I took balsa wood and wood burned all the images.

The Stand Outs

A coffee table book I designed about the history of Japanese Fashion. Using clean and simple design, which is prominent in modern Japanese design, I wanted to enhance interest in the book by providing a diverse and interesting layout, but keeping the readers focused on the information.

Ready Set Shop

A conceptual app that helps users get in and out of shopping centres as quickly as possible. The app parses information from the local WiFi connection of the centre and it only lists the shops that are currently having deals or sales. The user can also search by shops or products.

Steez TV

A motion graphics projects focused on creating a broadcast package for an existing channel/network or a fictional one. Steez specializes on showcasing TV shows that are about 'street' oriented activities.

Virtual Eyes

is an app that caters itself to two groups of people. Users and viewers. The app itself will be invitation based, like Dribble, when it comes to its users. However, anyone can download the app with limited actions to perform for the viewers. The main purpose of the app is to allow artists to use the city as there own personal canvas. Wether it would be graffiti, stencil work, abstract artwork, and motion graphics. The list goes on. The app lets the users take photos of a desired 'canvas' (a wall, the road, the side of a building ,whatever comes in mind) and upload, then mock up their work on that 'canvas'.

Jerick's Website

Branding and creating a website for Jerick Collantes' photography website. The minimalistic approach does not distract the viewer from the actual work.

Hannya Shift

Series of "Hannya" mask illustration. I have always been fascinated by the Japanese Culture and its theater mask. Now popularized by modern art/tattoos, I want to contribute to is popularity by overlaying the mask over world renowned statues

About

Hip Hop culture taught me that creativity is as vast as you see it to be. It has influenced me greatly and given me so much.
